Colocation & Disaster Recovery

Your Infrastructure,
Hosted in Hull

Jackson Systems operates a bespoke colocation facility in Kingston upon Hull, with direct upstream provider links to the internet backbone — delivering exceptional throughput and consistently low latency for hosted workloads.

Whether you need a resilient secondary site, a fully managed hosting environment, or a complete disaster recovery solution, our facility can replicate your entire systems estate — servers, storage, networking — and keep it ready to take over at a moment's notice.
