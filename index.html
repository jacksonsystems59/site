OUR STORY

Meet Chris Jackson

Jackson Technology Services is a leading provider of IT solutions, specialising in support and systems management for businesses of all sizes.

Technology plays a crucial role in the operations of most businesses today. Jackson Systems, founded by Chris Jackson in 2017, has extensive experience in helping both large and small businesses navigate the complexities of technology. Drawing on Chris’ decades of experience in the corporate and education sectors, the company offers a wide range of IT solutions, from helpdesk support to the deployment, implementation, and management of large-scale systems. With a strong reputation in the managed services provider (MSP) market, Jackson Systems has successfully supported businesses in various industries across multiple regions in the United Kingdom.

Our clients equate an unwavering IT Infrastructure to the efficient running of business, we are always by their side when they need our services the most.
