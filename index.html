Our Framework

The 12 Principles of
Good IT Health

Every business we support is assessed against our 12 Principles framework — a practical checklist that keeps your systems secure, compliant, and running at their best. Ongoing, not just at onboarding.

01

Endpoint Protection

Every device covered by active, managed security software — no gaps.

02

Patch & Update Management

OS and application patches applied promptly and systematically.

03

Access Control

Least-privilege accounts, MFA enforced, and regular access reviews.

04

Backup & Recovery

Tested, automated backups with documented recovery procedures.

05

Firewall & Perimeter

Properly configured firewalls with reviewed rules and no legacy exceptions.

06

Email Security

DMARC, DKIM, SPF configured. Anti-phishing filtering active.

07

Network Segmentation

Guest, corporate and IoT traffic separated — lateral movement contained.

08

Monitoring & Alerting

Proactive monitoring across infrastructure with meaningful alerts, not noise.

09

Compliance Alignment

Controls mapped to Cyber Essentials and GDPR requirements, kept current.

10

Incident Response

Documented plan, tested contacts, and clear escalation paths in place.

11

User Awareness

Staff trained to recognise phishing and social engineering attempts.

12

Continuous Improvement

Regular reviews, documented changes, and a roadmap that keeps pace with your business.
